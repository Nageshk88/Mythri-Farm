About Mytri Farm

A farm built for families, celebrations & togetherness

Mytri Farm is a spacious, nature-rooted destination near Ras Al Khaimah Airport — spread across 4 lush acres lined with date trees and open greenery. We're not a hotel and we're not a resort. We're a warm, affordable, family-friendly farm with dedicated spaces for quiet overnight stays, large outdoor events, and cosy indoor gatherings.

Whether you're planning a relaxed weekend with family, a birthday celebration for hundreds, or a private indoor dinner for 80 — Mytri Farm has a zone that fits, without the luxury price tag.
